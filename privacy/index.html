PRIVACY POLICY

At Kobe BCN, we are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This policy details how we process the data we collect or receive from you.

Data We Collect

We collect information such as your name, email address, and phone number when you request info, contact us, or register for reservation services via our platform.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to process table reservations via CoverManager, respond to inquiries, and manage site performance metrics via Google Analytics (once consent is granted).

Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, delete, or limit the processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please email us at Admin@arukubcn.com.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share necessary data only with authorized processors, such as CoverManager for restaurant reservations and Google Analytics for web usage insights.
